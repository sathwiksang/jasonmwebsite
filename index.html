About

A Disciplined Approach to Wealth Creation

I started my journey in real estate with mobile homes and quickly realized how powerful they are for generating cashflow and creating opportunities where others overlook them.

Since then, I've expanded into land investing, stocks, and building side businesses — all while learning which tools and strategies actually work in the real world.

From flipping single units in parks to structuring seller-financed deals, I've built a business around helping buyers and sellers find win-win solutions. My approach is practical: start small, scale smart, and keep learning.
